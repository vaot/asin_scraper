From the manufacturer

Teether. Baby Finger Sized.

Our teethers designed to mimic those little baby fingers and are made with hygienic silicone material thats safe, and oh so perfectly chewy. Quite simply, theyre made to give you hours of silent bliss.

Sensible Material

When it comes to all things baby, we agree that the single most important thing is safety. After exploring various options, we found out that silicone was safe.

Intuitive Design

You dont have to look much further than the mess at meal time to know that your little one hasnt quite perfected his hand-to-eye coordination. So we simplified the design to make our teethers easy and intuitive to grab and hold. The four prongs are perfectly sized to mimic baby finger. So theyre just long enough to reach the back molars, but are sensibly sized to prevent choking.

  • Specification: Our teethers come in one size and are available in two colors (Orange, Blue).
  • It features a one-piece design

In coming up with our concept, we were inspired to go back to the fundamentals. The truth is, your baby already has the most thoughtfully designed teething toy out there. It just so happens to be attached to their hands. So we simply copied from the best with just a bit of a twist.
